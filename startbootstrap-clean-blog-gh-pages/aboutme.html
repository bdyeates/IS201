Why are running shoes important?

Something that is very important if you are trying to get into running is having a reliable pair of running shoes. Throughout my years of running, it has taken many many pairs of running shoes to finally find the ones for me and it has made all of the difference. One thing I recommend is going to a running shoe store where the employees can help analyze your feet for a shoe that will be the perfect fit for your foot width, arch, running needs, etc.
