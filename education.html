Education

I absolutely hated it and after 3 months of interning, I decided that was not what I wanted to do. I decided that since I loved math and it is something that I was passionate about and good at, that I wanted to major in engineering.

That is how I came across mechanical engineering. Because of this major shift, I decided that it was best for me to also switch colleges to somewhere that had an amazing engineering program and that is how I landed on BYU.
